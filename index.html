About Talken

Talken Is A Pioneering Chat Platform Designed To Redefine The Way We Perceive Privacy In Digital Communication. Developed With Decentralization And Data Sovereignty At Its Core, Talken Offers A Seamless Experience Backed By Your Own Cloud Storage.

It Is A Completely New Realm Of Communication Applications, Where Your Privacy Is Simply Guaranteed. This Makes Talken An Ideal Choice For Journalists, Content Creators, Diplomats, And Others Who Value Discretion.

Talken Can Send Any File Of Size Up To 15 GB Or The Available Space In Your Google Drive, Which Is Unmatched By Most Other Chat Applications. All Media Sent Through Talken Is Preserved In Its Original Size And Quality.
