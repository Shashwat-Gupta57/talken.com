About Talken

Talken is a chat platform built around privacy and data sovereignty. Instead of routing your conversations through a central service, it connects peers directly and keeps your data in storage you own.

There is no central chat server to trust, subpoena, or breach. That design makes Talken well suited to anyone who values discretion — journalists, creators, and privacy-conscious users alike.

Files are limited only by your own Google Drive space, and every message and attachment is preserved at its original size and quality — no compression, no caps imposed by us.

* 3rd‑party storage
