Your Messages, Your Control

Talken is a minimalist chat app built by a solo developer, designed to put privacy first.

How Talken Works:

  • 🔐 Zero-Server Storage - Messages are backed up to your Google Drive, not our servers
  • 🤖 No Ads, No Tracking - Built for communication, not surveillance
  • 🔄 Mandatory Backups - Automatic Google Drive sync ensures you never lose chats
  • 📁 Limit-less Storage - You can send as many messages and files as you want, Talken will never limit you down
  • 👨‍💻 One-Person Mission - Developed independently by Shashwat Gupta of Flex Studios
