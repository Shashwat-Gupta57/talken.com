1. Information We Collect

Talken is designed to minimize data collection. We only process:

  • Talken Account Credentials (email/password for app login).
  • Google Drive Access Token (only to backup chats/messages locally to your Google Drive).
  • Group IDs & Member Lists (necessary for group messaging functionality, stored on our servers).

No messages, media, or personal data are stored on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Google Drive Access: Solely to automatically backup your chats/messages to your own Google Drive (mandatory for app functionality).
  • Group IDs: To manage group memberships and deliver messages.
  • Account Credentials: To authenticate your Talken account.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • All backups are stored locally on your Google Drive (we cannot access them).
  • Group IDs/member lists are stored on our servers (no encryption yet, but it will be added soon).
  • Account data is deleted permanently upon user request (no recovery possible).

4. Third-Party Services

The following third-party service is used:

  • Google Drive API (required for backups; you must sign in via Google).

No ads, analytics, or payment processors are used.

5. Data Sharing & Selling

We do NOT share, sell, or monetize your data in any way.

Google Drive data is fully controlled by you (we only request access to save backups).

6. User Rights & Deletion

  • You can delete your Talken account anytime (erases all server-stored data permanently).
  • To revoke Google Drive access, remove Talken’s permissions via your Google Account settings.

7. Age Restrictions

Talken is safe for all ages, including children (no harmful content/data collection).

8. Policy Updates

During development, this policy may change without prior notice.

Post-launch, users will be notified of updates via email/app alerts.

Contact Us

For data deletion requests or questions, email: talken.flexstudios@gmail.com.

Google Drive API Compliance Note

This app uses Google Drive API only for user-authorized backups. By signing in, you grant Talken limited access (read/write) only to the app’s designated backup folder. Google’s Privacy Policy applies to Drive data: Google Privacy Policy.
