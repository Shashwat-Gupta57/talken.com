Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Talken, developed by Flex Studios. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our chat application. By using Talken, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • The email address you provide to create and sign in to your Talken account
  • Google Drive access (via OAuth) used only to back up your messages and media to your own Drive — your Drive contents are never read by us
  • Profile information you choose to share (display name, about section)
  • Contact information for support purposes

2.2 Usage Data

  • Application performance metrics
  • Error logs and crash reports
  • Feature usage statistics (anonymized)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining the Talken service
  • Backing up your messages and media to your own Google Drive at your request
  • Improving application performance and user experience
  • Providing customer support
  • Detecting and preventing technical issues

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Zero-Server Storage Policy

Your messages and files are NEVER stored on our servers. All communication data is:

  • Stored directly in your personal Google Drive
  • Encrypted during transmission
  • Accessible only by you and your intended recipients
  • Under your complete control and ownership

4.2 Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for all communications
  • Secure Google OAuth 2.0 authentication
  • No third-party data sharing
  • Regular security audits and updates

5. Third-Party Services

Talken integrates with:

  • Google Drive API: For message and file storage
  • Google OAuth: For secure authentication

These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

6. Data Retention

  • Messages and files: Stored in your Google Drive indefinitely (until you delete them)
  • Application logs: Retained for 30 days for debugging purposes
  • Account information: Retained while your account is active

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data (by deleting from Google Drive)
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

8. Children's Privacy

Talken is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: talken.flexstudios@gmail.com
  • Developer: Shashwat Gupta, Flex Studios

🔒 Privacy Promise

Talken was built with privacy as the foundation. We don't sell your data, we don't show ads, and we don't store your messages. Your conversations belong to you, and only you.
