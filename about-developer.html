The Coding Journey

From hating programming to building revolutionary software - a transformation story.

Oct 2023

The Turning Point

Until my 9th grade half-yearly exams, I genuinely despised coding. It seemed pointless, complex, and utterly boring. But on November 8th, 2023, a simple idea changed everything - what if I could create a character that moves across a terminal screen based on user input?

Nov 2023

First Breakthrough

Using nothing but BlueJ - a beginner IDE that struggles with codebases over 1000 lines - I created my first program. A simple character spawner that placed alphanumeric characters at specific positions. Then I evolved it into an 8-line player character, added animations by clearing the terminal every 500ms, and made it walk across the screen. All within a month.

Late 2023

First GUI Applications

The terminal wasn't enough anymore. I created my first GUI application - a Click Per Second tester. Then came my first game: Rex Run, inspired by Chrome's dinosaur game and Geometry Dash. The addiction to creating had begun.

2024

The Creative Explosion

What followed was an unstoppable creative streak. I built 16 more applications: Ping Pong Game, Flying Dual (local multiplayer), Flappy Bird, Memo (Simon game replica), Tic Tac Toe, SG Paint (MS Paint replica), Notes (superior to Windows Notepad in many aspects), four different widgets (Weather, Clock, Mixed, and Transparent Clock), a JRE Installer, a floating keyboard, and a color picker.

Jan 2024

The Talken Vision

The idea for Talken was born - a privacy-first chat application that would revolutionize how we think about digital communication. But I needed to discover the right technologies first.

Dec 2024

Technology Discovery

After months of research and learning, I finally found the perfect combination of technologies to bring Talken to life. The vision was clear, the tools were ready.

Apr 2024

Academic Excellence & Resume

After achieving 95% in my final board exams, I resumed serious work on Talken on April 11th, 2024. The academic success gave me the confidence to pursue my dream project full-time.

May 2024

Talken Launch

On May 22nd, 2024, the first official version of Talken was ready. 17,000 lines of carefully crafted code, representing months of dedication, innovation, and the culmination of my coding journey.
